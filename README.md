Email templates used by Stratton Index for the ILS facilitator certification process.

**Live previews (no login required):**
- [Facilitator Welcome Email](https://philipdns.github.io/stratton-index-ils-product/Facilitator%20Welcome%20Email.html) — standard onboarding, 5 steps
- [Facilitator Welcome Email — Post-Survey](https://philipdns.github.io/stratton-index-ils-product/Facilitator%20Welcome%20Email%20-%20Post-Survey.html) — for facilitators who already took the ILS, 4 steps

## Files

### `Facilitator Welcome Email.html`

Use for facilitators who have **not** previously taken the ILS survey. Full 5-step certification path.

**Attachments to include when sending:**
- Stratton Index Facilitator Guide (PDF)
- ILS Workshop Deck ×2 (PPTX)
- ILS Debrief Deck (PPTX)
- Recipient's ILS Report (PDF)
- ILS Licensing Agreement (PDF)

---

### `Facilitator Welcome Email - Post-Survey.html`

Use for facilitators who **already completed the ILS survey** through another facilitator. Skips Step 1 (the survey) — 4 remaining steps.

**Attachments to include when sending:**
- Stratton Index Facilitator Guide (PDF)
- ILS Workshop Deck ×2 (PPTX)
- ILS Debrief Deck (PPTX)
- ILS Licensing Agreement (PDF)
- *(No ILS report needed — they already have it)*

---

## How to use either template

- Open the preview link above in any browser to see the rendered email.
- Copy the email body and paste it into Gmail, Outlook, etc.
- Replace the mail-merge placeholders before sending:
  - `{{FirstName}}` — recipient's first name
  - `{{LastName}}` — recipient's last name
  - `{{Email}}` — recipient's email address
- The "From / To / Cc / Attached" header above the email card is a preview aid only — do not copy it.
