Your certification path
Five steps, in this order.

Each step builds on the last. The full path takes roughly two to three hours of your time, spread across whatever schedule works for you.
1. Complete the ILS survey  · download your results and materials, then review both ~10 min
2. Complete the ILS E-Course  · short videos and scenarios that deepen your reading of the survey ~20 min
3. Review the Facilitator’s Guide  · the canonical reference for delivering ILS sessions 60–90 min
4. Watch the ILS Quick Tip Videos  · short, practical highlights for delivery and facilitation ~30 min
5. Schedule your one-hour Q&A with Philip  · the final step — reach out once you’ve completed the above 60 min

For later · running your first session
When you’re ready to bring the ILS to your clients

Once you’re certified, you’ll order surveys for your groups through our online order form. Survey fees are $25 per participant with digital delivery of all reports and materials. Change fees apply at $25 per incident — see your licensing agreement for specifics.

How an order flows
1.  Complete the order form and upload your participant list (first & last names).
2.  We create the group and send you the link, code, and participant instructions — usually within 24 hours.
3.  We send completion updates while the group is open (we recommend 10–14 days).
4.  Individual and group reports are emailed to you the day after the group closes.
Tips when filling out the form
Event date The date of your session.
Arrive-by date The date you need reports in hand.
Open date When the survey opens for participants.
Close date Set this at least two days before your arrive-by date — reports go out the day after close, and we operate on EST.
Participants First and last names only, Excel is great. Decide whether participants can download their own results at completion — that gives them access before your session.
